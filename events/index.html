Industry-Academia meet

As many of you know, there has been a huge surge of research interest in the domain of quantum computation and quantum technology. The Govt. of India also has recently laid down a plan for spending 6000 Cr. rupees for building quantum technology in India. The rapid growth of quantum technologies has created numerous job opportunities in private sectors as well, in India and abroad, and the trend is going only upwards. Entrepreneurship in Quantum Technologies is also flourishing faster than ever.
In light of this ongoing second quantum revolution, we are arranging an online Industry-Academia meet-up with the Quantum Industry Stalwarts of India. We encourage all members, especially young students to attend the event who are interested in being part of this technology revolution in India. You will have the opportunity to know what is going on in the quantum industries and interact with the speakers.

Researchers Meet

Quantum Information and Quantum Technology - 2023 (QIQT -2023), marks the fourth workshop in a series conducted during the summer months at the renowned Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Kolkata (IISER-K). Its primary objective is to facilitate face-to-face interactions between leading experts and the next generation of students and young researchers. With a diverse array of participants including Physicists, Chemists, Mathematicians, Computer Scientists, and Engineers, QIQT -2023 promises to be a multidisciplinary platform fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange.
During last week of the QIQT-2023, we offer a platform for young researchers to showcase their own contributions, fostering recognition and encouragement for their valuable work.

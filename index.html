About the Event

Quantum Information Technologies with Photonic Devices is currently a very active area of research. It focuses on performing quantum information and quantum communication tasks using photons. In addition, this theme studies the foundations of quantum mechanics in general and quantum entanglement in particular in order to explore novel quantum information applications.
Step into the quantum world with us as we kick off the QuEST (Quantum Enabled Science & Technology) Theme 1 Symposium. This digital platform serves as the gateway to a realm where photons, entanglement, and quantum technologies converge to redefine the boundaries of scientific discovery. QuEST Theme 1, with its 24 pioneering projects scattered across 17 esteemed research institutes in India, is at the forefront of Quantum Information Technologies with Photonic Devices.
